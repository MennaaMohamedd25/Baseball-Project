Smith Hired in Toronto

Sunday, April 3rd , 2022

Jim Smith has now officially taken over as GM and manager of the Toronto Blue Jays.

"There's a lot of faith in him to do the job, but I'm not so sure how much rope he will get," said a Major League Baseball source close to the team, who wished to stay anonymous.

"They're really counting on him to do the job," he added about the 45-year-old out of The United States.

BNN Top 100 Prospects List Published

Sunday, April 3rd , 2022

The season is about to start, so it's time to publish the annual BNN Top 100 Prospects list. This year, 21 year-old CF Julio Rodriguez of the Seattle Mariners is the #1 prospect overall..

Here is the top 10:

  1. CF Julio Rodriguez , 21, Seattle Mariners
  2. C Adley Rutschman , 24, Baltimore Orioles
  3. 3B Bobby Witt Jr. , 21, Kansas City Royals
  4. RF Riley Greene , 21, Detroit Tigers
  5. C Gabriel Morenoe , 22, Toronto Blue Jays
  6. RHP Shane Baz , 22, Tampa Bay Rays
  7. RHP Hunter Greene , 22, Cincinnati Reds
  8. RHP Grayson Rodriguez , 22, Baltimore Orioles
  9. C Francisco Alvarez , 20, New York Mets
  10. 3B Jordan Walker , 19, St. Louis Cardinals

You can view the full list in the Top Prospects Report from the reports menu. The Top Minor League Systems Report has been updated as well.
